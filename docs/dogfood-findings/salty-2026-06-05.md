## Confirmed FALSE POSITIVES (precision-backlog signal)
1. **FlashLoanGovernance @ `Proposals.sol:259 castVote`** ‚Äî FP. Voting power = xSALT, acquired only via `Staking.stakeSALT` (real SALT transferred in) and exited only via `unstake` ‚Üí a `PENDING` Unstake with `completionTime ‚â• block.timestamp + 2 weeks` (early exit burns ‚â•80%). xSALT is non-transferable. No atomic stake‚Üívote‚Üíunstake‚Üírepay path, so a flash loan can't be repaid in-tx. The detector flags live (non-snapshotted) voting power, which is a reasonable heuristic but is defeated here by the timelock-unstake + non-transferability ‚Äî needs cross-contract reasoning to suppress.
2. **TwapManipulation @ `CoreUniswapFeed.sol:50 _getUniswapTwapWei`** ‚Äî FP. Uses Uniswap v3 `observe()` over a fixed 30-min `TWAP_PERIOD` (manipulation-resistant), AND it's only one of three feeds in `PriceAggregator._aggregatePrices`, which takes the closest two of {Uniswap-TWAP, Chainlink, Salty-spot}, averages, and reverts if they diverge >3%. A single corrupted feed is discarded as the outlier.

## Medium ‚Äî first-depositor steals rewards accrued to an empty pool
- **Locations:** `StakingRewards.sol:182 addSALTRewards` (`totalRewards[poolID] += amountToAdd`, no `totalShares>0` guard) + `StakingRewards.sol:78 _increaseUserShare` (virtual-rewards offset applied only `if (existingTotalShares != 0)`).
- **Invariant:** a user's claimable rewards must equal rewards accrued *while they held a share*.
- **Trigger:** DAO whitelisting a token (`DAO._finalizeTokenWhitelisting:235`) immediately sends `bootstrappingRewards` into the emitter, dripping into `totalRewards` for pools that may have zero stakers; `addSALTRewards` is also public. The first LP enters with `existingTotalShares==0` ‚Üí no virtualRewards charged ‚Üí `userRewardForPool` credits them 100% of the pre-existing `totalRewards`.
- **Impact:** seed/bootstrap rewards meant for early LPs collectively are siphoned by a single first-mover. Bounded by the per-pool reward in the empty window (cooldown 1‚Äì6h + 1%/day emission drip cap it). Value-misallocation, not principal loss ‚Üí Medium.
- **Fix:** in `addSALTRewards`, if `totalShares[poolID]==0` route/defer the reward; or seed a minimum locked share (MINIMUM_LIQUIDITY pattern) so a live pool's `existingTotalShares` is never 0.
